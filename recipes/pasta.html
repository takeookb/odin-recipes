Lemon Pasta

Lemon Pasta Sauce

it's creamy and wonderful and it's lemon and lime.

Serve with cubed cooked meat or veggies,if desired.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon grated lime zest
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 10 ounces uncooked pasta

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan or skillet large enough to hold the pasta when it is done, combine the

    nutter, cream, and beef broth or bouillon and simmer over medium heat until

    reduced by aboout half. Add the lemon juice, lemon and lime zest and set aside

  2. bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain. Toss pasta with sauce; serve.
